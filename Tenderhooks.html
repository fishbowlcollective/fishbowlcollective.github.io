An excavation into the hidden absurdities that lie beneath everyday life - three clowns chip away at the surface of three teenage minds to expose the enigmatic world that passes by unnoticed day to day. Through physical theatre and collective creation, Tenderhooks dives into the subconscious to expose the hidden beauty that is the young brain. First produced at the 2013 Paprika Festival.
