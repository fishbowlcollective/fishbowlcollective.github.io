An Ode to Dyads

Two beings. Doomed to forever repeat their existence in a strange and disquieting world where chairs are loyal companions, exalted french poets dictate emotion, and narrative is swallowed by metaphysical clowns.

This piece was conceived and presented at the 2014 Paprika Festival, was chosen to represent the festival at the 2014 Toronto Fringe, later went on to the Victoria Fringe, and has been shown in highschools and theatres in Ontario, Quebec,and Nova Scotia.

"It is impressive and inspiring to see young people creating evocative and satisfying work. Fishbowl Collective is definitely a group to watch out for"
-Mooney on Theatre

"[An] Ode to Dyads is a gorgeous poem of sorts that brings wit and heartbreak to an unexpected and very human medium, whether your heart beats for dance or not"
-CVV Magazine

"Truly theatrical. One of the most inventive, exciting new works I have seen in awhile"
-Lynda Hill (Artistic Director, Theater Direct)
