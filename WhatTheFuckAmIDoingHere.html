What the Fuck am I Doing Here? is an anti-folk opera which follows the timeline of the Quebec student uprising told through the eyes of an anglo student from outside of Quebec - a barista, a feminist, and an anarchist song-writer - who becomes involved in the movement. She guides us through the ups and downs of being a part-time waitress, part-time revolutionary, involved with grassroots organizing in Montreal; giving voice to both the struggles and the inspiration involved with making the world a better place.
